# DMCA Copyright Policy

**Last Updated: January 2024**

GuardiaVault respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users to do the same. This policy outlines our procedures for handling copyright infringement claims under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

## 1. Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed on GuardiaVault, please provide the following information to our designated agent:

### 1.1 Required Information
- **Identification**: Description of copyrighted work
- **Location**: Exact location of infringing material (URL, page)
- **Contact**: Your contact information (name, address, phone, email)
- **Statement**: Good faith belief that use is not authorized
- **Accuracy**: Statement that information is accurate
- **Authorization**: Statement that you are authorized to act on behalf of copyright owner
- **Signature**: Physical or electronic signature

### 1.2 How to Submit
**Email**: dmca@guardiavault.com  
**Subject**: DMCA Copyright Infringement Notice

## 2. Counter-Notification

If you believe content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification.

### 2.1 Required Information
- **Identification**: Description of removed content
- **Location**: Where content was located
- **Statement**: Good faith belief content was removed by mistake
- **Consent**: Consent to jurisdiction of federal court
- **Contact**: Your contact information
- **Signature**: Physical or electronic signature

## 3. Repeat Infringers

Users who repeatedly infringe copyrights may have their accounts terminated.

**DMCA Agent**: dmca@guardiavault.com  
**Legal**: legal@guardiavault.com

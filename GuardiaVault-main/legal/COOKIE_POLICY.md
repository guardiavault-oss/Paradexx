# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how GuardiaVault ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our platform.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us:
- Remember your preferences
- Improve user experience
- Analyze website usage
- Provide security features

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential Cookies (Required)
**Purpose**: Necessary for platform functionality

**Examples**:
- Session management
- Authentication
- Security features
- Load balancing

**Duration**: Session or 30 days

**Can You Disable**: No - these are required for the platform to function

### 3.2 Functional Cookies (Optional)
**Purpose**: Enhance functionality

**Examples**:
- Remember language preferences
- Remember theme preferences
- Remember user interface settings

**Duration**: Up to 1 year

**Can You Disable**: Yes - but features may not work properly

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies (Optional)
**Purpose**: Understand how users interact with our platform

**Examples**:
- Page views
- Time spent on pages
- Navigation patterns
- Error tracking (anonymized)

**Duration**: Up to 2 years

**Can You Disable**: Yes - via browser settings or our preference center

**Third-Party**: Sentry (error tracking)

### 3.4 Marketing Cookies (Optional)
**Purpose**: Deliver relevant advertisements (if applicable)

**Examples**:
- Remarketing
- Conversion tracking
- Ad personalization

**Duration**: Varies

**Can You Disable**: Yes - via browser settings

**Currently**: We do not use marketing cookies

## 6. Managing Cookies

### 6.1 Browser Settings
Most browsers allow you to:
- View cookies
- Delete cookies
- Block cookies
- Set cookie preferences

**Browser Guides**:
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

### 6.2 Our Preference Center
- Access via account settings
- Control optional cookies
- Essential cookies cannot be disabled

### 6.3 Do Not Track
- We respect browser "Do Not Track" signals
- However, essential cookies are still required
- Analytics may be disabled if DNT is enabled

## 7. Cookie Duration

### 7.1 Session Cookies
- Deleted when you close your browser
- Used for temporary data
- Examples: Login sessions, form data

### 7.2 Persistent Cookies
- Remain until expiration or deletion
- Used for preferences and analytics
- Examples: Language, theme, analytics

## 8. Local Storage and Similar Technologies

### 8.1 Local Storage
We use browser local storage (similar to cookies) for:
- User preferences
- Cache data
- Offline functionality

**You can clear local storage** via browser settings.

### 8.2 Web Beacons
We may use web beacons (small images) in emails to:
- Track email opens
- Measure campaign effectiveness
- Improve email delivery

## 9. Mobile Apps

### 9.1 React Native App
- Uses device storage instead of cookies
- Similar tracking for analytics
- You can disable analytics in app settings

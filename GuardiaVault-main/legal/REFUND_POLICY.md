# Refund Policy

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## 1. General Policy

GuardiaVault operates on an annual subscription model. This refund policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued.

## 2. Subscription Refunds

### 2.1 Annual Subscriptions
- **Basic Plan**: $99/year
- **Family Plan**: $199/year  
- **Premium Plan**: $499/year

### 2.2 Refund Eligibility
Refunds are **generally not available** for:
- Unused subscription periods
- Early cancellation
- Change of mind
- Failure to use the service
- Vault creation issues (unless due to platform error)

### 2.3 Refund Exceptions
Refunds may be issued in the following circumstances:

**a) Service Non-Performance**
- Platform is completely non-functional for extended periods (7+ days)
- Critical security breach affecting your account
- Data loss due to platform error

**b) Billing Errors**
- Incorrect charges
- Duplicate charges
- Unauthorized charges (after verification)

**c) Cancellation Within 14 Days (EU/UK Users)**
- European Union and UK users have 14-day cooling-off period
- Full refund if cancelled within 14 days of initial subscription
- Must not have created any vaults (or vaults must be deleted)

**d) Legal Requirements**
- Refunds required by applicable law
- Court orders or legal settlements

## 3. Processing Fees

### 3.1 Payment Processing
- Payments processed via Stripe
- Refunds processed back to original payment method
- Processing time: 5-10 business days

### 3.2 Non-Refundable Fees
- **Gas Fees**: Blockchain transaction fees are non-refundable
- **Third-Party Fees**: Any fees charged by payment processors are non-refundable
- **Currency Conversion**: Exchange rate differences are non-refundable

## 4. Partial Refunds

### 4.1 Prorated Refunds
Prorated refunds are **not available** except:
- Required by law
- Platform error causing service disruption
- Security breach affecting your account

### 4.2 Service Period
- Cancellation takes effect at end of paid period
- No refunds for partial periods
- Access continues until subscription expires

## 5. Chargebacks

### 5.1 Chargeback Policy
- Chargebacks are considered account violations
- Accounts with chargebacks will be immediately suspended
- We reserve the right to pursue legal action for fraudulent chargebacks

### 5.2 Resolution Process
- Contact support before initiating chargeback
- We will work to resolve disputes amicably
- Chargebacks may result in permanent account ban

## 6. Refund Request Process

### 6.1 How to Request
1. Email: refunds@guardiavault.com
2. Include:
   - Account email
   - Subscription plan
   - Reason for refund request
   - Transaction ID or receipt

### 6.2 Review Process
- Requests reviewed within 5 business days
- We may request additional information
- Decisions are final unless required by law

### 6.3 Approval
- Approved refunds processed within 10 business days
- Funds returned to original payment method
- You will receive email confirmation

## 7. Non-Refundable Items

### 7.1 Services
- Subscription fees (except as outlined above)
- Gas fees for blockchain transactions
- Professional service fees (if offered)
- Custom development (if offered)

### 7.2 Circumstances
- Change of mind after 14 days
- Failure to understand service
- Failure to read terms and conditions
- User error (wrong configuration, etc.)
- Guardian or beneficiary issues
- Death verification delays

## 8. Subscription Cancellation

### 8.1 How to Cancel
- Through account settings
- Email: support@guardiavault.com
- Cancellation is immediate for future billing

### 8.2 Effect of Cancellation
- Current period access continues
- No refund for current period
- Data retention per Privacy Policy
- Vaults remain accessible until subscription expires

## 9. Force Majeure

We are not responsible for refunds due to:
- Natural disasters
- War or terrorism
- Government actions
- Blockchain network failures
- Third-party service failures

## 10. Dispute Resolution

### 10.1 Internal Resolution
- All refund requests reviewed internally first
- We aim for fair resolution
- Decisions based on policy and circumstances

### 10.2 Legal Rights
- You retain all legal rights
- This policy does not waive statutory rights
- Consult legal counsel if needed

## 11. Special Circumstances

### 11.1 Medical Emergencies
- Considered on case-by-case basis
- Documentation may be required
- Not guaranteed

### 11.2 Death
- Beneficiaries may request refunds
- Requires death certificate and proof of relationship
- Prorated refunds may be considered

### 11.3 Military Deployment
- Active military deployment may qualify
- Requires proof of deployment
- Contact support for consideration

## 12. Updates to Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
